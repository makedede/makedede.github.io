Biography

     Kede Ma is an Assistant Professor with the Department of Computer Science at City University of Hong Kong (CityUHK). He received the B.E. degree from the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2012, the MASc. and Ph.D. degrees from the University of Waterloo, in 2014 and 2017, respectively. Prior to joining CityUHK, he was a Research Associate with Howard Hughes Medical Institute and New York University, from 2018 to 2019. Prof. Ma has been named to the Highly Cited Researchers list by Clarivate Analytics, in 2024. He also serves on the Editorial Boards of IEEE Transactions on Image Processing and IEEE Transactions on Information Forensics and Security.
Prof. Ma leads the Multimedia Analytics (MA) Laboratory, which is an interdisciplinary research group. We are interested in
  • Computational Vision
    • Computational Modeling of the Human Visual Perception
    • Perceptual Multimedia Signal Processing and Quality Assessment
    • Multimedia Quality-of-Experience (QoE)
  • Computational Photography
    • High Dynamic Range (HDR) Imaging and Rendering
    • Virtual Reality (VR) Video Representation, Analysis, and Streaming
    • Camera Processing Pipeline Design and Optimization
    • Generative Modeling of Multimedia Signals
  • Multimedia Forensics and Security
    • Multimedia Information Hiding
    • Multimedia Forgery Detection
    • Privacy-Preserving Multimedia Data Analysis
    • Artificial Intelligence (AI) Safety
