Notes to Prospective Students

     We are always seeking for "top-performing" students to join our research team. Please send me an email if you are interested, with your CV, transcript and a brief research statement. The "performance" is qualitatively measured by 1) background in mathematics, 2) skill in computer programming, and 3) intuition in working on research projects.

     We welcome highly self-motivated undergraduates at CityU to conduct research with us. Self-funded visiting scholars are also welcome to apply.
